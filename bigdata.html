What is Big Data?

Introduction

What is Big Data?
"Big data" is the massive amount of data available to organizations that—because of its volume and complexity—is not easily managed or analyzed by many business intelligence tools.
Tools for big data can help with the volume of the data collected, the speed at which that data becomes available to an organization for analysis, and the complexity or varieties of that data.
